About Us

We Are Bakelore

WE LOVE CAKE

Bakelore was founded with the aim of bringing scrumpdillylicious traditional baked goods to modern-day customers. We believe that good food should be made with the finest ingredients and from the finest memories of the '90s with our families.

We are passionate about bringing former-time delicacies by inculcating our recipes with nostalgic flavors and combinations. Bakelore strives to create an atmosphere that is warm and welcoming, which is why nostalgia is set as the core theme to bring good food and good memories from the past together. We hope that Bakelore becomes a place where you come to enjoy delicious food and reminisce memories with friends and family.
